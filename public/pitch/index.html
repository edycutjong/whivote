The Problem

DAO governance is slow, boring, and expensive.

  • ⏰ 7-day voting periods — Proposals sit for a week while the market moves
  • 💤 <10% participation — Long votes create apathy; most token holders ignore proposals
  • 💸 $2–5 per vote — On-chain votes on L1 cost real money, discouraging small holders

MakerDAO averaged 5% voter turnout in 2024. $1.3B protocol, governed by a handful.

The Solution

60-second ephemeral polls. Gas-free via MagicBlock. Real-time results.

⚡

Ephemeral Sessions

MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollups spin up vote sessions. 60s TTL. Auto-finalize.

🗳️

Wallet-Gated Polls

📊

Real-Time Results
